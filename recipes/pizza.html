BBQ Chicken Pizza

This BBQ chicken pizza has spicy barbecue sauce, diced chicken, peppers, onion, and cilantro, all covered with cheese and baked to bubbly goodness! This is similar to a recipe I had at a popular pizza place in California. My family loves it!

Ingredients

  • 1(12inch) pre-baked pizza crust
  • 1 cup spicy barbeque sauce
  • 2 skinless boneless chiken breast halves, cooked and cubed
  • 1 cup sliced pepperoncini peppers
  • 1 cup chopped red onion
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh cliantro
  • 2 cups shredded Colby-Jack cheese

Instructions

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat the over to 350 degrtees F(175 degrees C)
  2. Place pizza crust on a baking sheet. Spread barbeque sauce on crust
  3. Top with ckicken, pepperoncini peppers, onion, and cilantro
  4. Cover with Colby-Jack cheese
  5. Back in the breheated oven until chees is melted and bubbly, about 15mins
